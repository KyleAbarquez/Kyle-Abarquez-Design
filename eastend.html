East End Entertainment

Website Design & Development

My Role: Planning, Concepting, Design, Development, Deployment
Tools Used: Sketch, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Project Summary
My freelance partner and I teamed up on this project. East End Entertainment is a video production company. They came to me looking for a completely custom website to showcase the videos they have produced.

This was a fun opportunity for me to think outside the box design wise - since style was able to take precedent in this project. The most challenging part was definitely the development. It was coded from the ground by my partner and I.
Design Concepts
My partner and I submitted several designs for review by the company owners. Pictured below are the designs I did. Clear and stylish display of the videos and relevant information was the focus. Sleek navigation of the content was something else we were adamant about implementing.

Upon their review and feedback, we implemented the styles that they liked the most - combining certain aspects of different designs in order to create the design in the final product.
